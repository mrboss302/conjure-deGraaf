Privacy

Privacy at deGraaf.

Last updated: June 21, 2026

deGraaf builds Conjure, an AI website studio. This notice explains what we collect when you join the Conjure waitlist, why we collect it, and the choices you have. We keep this short on purpose — we only collect what we need to invite you to the beta.

What we collect

When you join the waitlist, you choose to share:

  • Your name and email address.
  • Your platform interest (e.g. macOS) and role.
  • Optionally, a short note about what you're building.
  • Whether you've opted in to give feedback that helps shape the product.

We do not collect payment information, and we don't use third-party advertising or tracking cookies on this site.

How we use it

  • To send you a TestFlight invitation when a beta seat opens.
  • To send occasional updates about Conjure's beta and launch.
  • If you opted in, to reach out for product feedback.

Who processes it

Waitlist signups are stored and emailed through Loops, our email and audience provider, acting on our behalf. The site is hosted on Cloudflare. We don't sell or rent your information to anyone.

Your choices

Every email we send includes an unsubscribe link. You can ask us to access or delete your information at any time by emailing hello@degraaf.app, and we'll take care of it.

Changes

If this notice changes, we'll update the date above. Material changes that affect how we use your information will be communicated by email where appropriate.

Questions? Email hello@degraaf.app. See also our Terms.
