How Conjure works

From first spark to a site
you can stand behind.

Conjure is a guided studio, not a one-shot generator. Each step keeps you in the chair — reading, editing, and steering as your site takes shape.

  1. 01

    Describe the site

    Tell Conjure what you're building, who it's for, and the tone you want. A sentence is enough to begin.

  2. 02

    Conjure drafts the structure

    It routes your idea and lays out the pages, sections, and copy around it — a real starting point, fast.

  3. 03

    Edit the copy as it lands

    Content arrives before the design does. Read it, rewrite it, make it yours while the visual system builds.

  4. 04

    Watch the design arrive

    The plain draft transforms into a composed, browser-true preview — type, color, layout, and rhythm.

  5. 05

    Refine with the assistant

    Ask for changes in plain language. Polish a section, warm up a call to action, rework the hero.

  6. 06

    Build out and prepare to launch

    Add pages, refine sections, and shape a site that's ready to share when you are.

The studio workspace

A creative command center, not a form.

Everything sits in one calm surface: your pages on the left, a live browser-true preview in the center, and an assistant on the right that answers what this is, what you can do, and what to try next.
